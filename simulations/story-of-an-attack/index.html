Story of an Attack - Mapping the Crime Scene

Welcome to the hot seat. When an active breach hits, chaos is your biggest enemy—you will easily find yourself drowning in a sea of disconnected logs, flashing alerts, and organizational panic. You cannot fight an attacker you cannot map. Use the Timeline Lens (Cyber Kill Chain) to pinpoint exactly how far the adversary has penetrated your network so you can run containment protocols before time runs out. Simultaneously, use the Relational Lens (Diamond Model) to instantly organize scattered pieces of evidence to see how the attacker's tools and assets connect. Mastering these frameworks moves you from a panicked defender blindly playing whack-a-mole with alerts to a tactical analyst who commands the battlefield.
